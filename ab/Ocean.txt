Oceans cover about 71% of Earth’s surface and are vital for life. There are five major oceans — the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Arctic. They regulate climate, produce much of the planet’s oxygen, and are home to countless species of plants and animals. Oceans also provide food, transport routes, energy resources, and support global trade. However, they face threats like pollution, overfishing, and climate change, making their conservation essential for the future.
